Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my college bus?

You can track your college bus using our website by entering your bus number or route details.

What should I do if I lose my belongings on the bus?

If you lose your belongings on the bus, please contact our support team immediately with details of the lost item and the bus route.

How do I report a problem with the bus service?

You can report any issues with the bus service through our contact form, email, or by calling our support number. Please provide as much detail as possible.

Can I book tickets through Bus Bee Track?

Currently, Bus Bee Track does not offer ticket booking services. We focus on providing real-time tracking and updates for your convenience.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes, our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play.

How do I update my contact information?

You can update your contact information by logging into your account on our website and navigating to the profile section.
