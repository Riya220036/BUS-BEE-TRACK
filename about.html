About BusBeeTrack

BusBeeTrack is an innovative project aimed at improving transportation safety and efficiency for college students. By leveraging modern technology, BusBeeTrack provides real-time tracking of buses, ensuring that students can plan their commutes effectively and safely.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to enhance the transportation experience for college students by providing a reliable and efficient bus tracking system. We aim to improve safety, reduce waiting times, and ensure that students can travel with peace of mind.
